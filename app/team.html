Hi! I'm David Gageot

My passion ? Writing complex yet simple software. Trying to be a facilitator helping teams being more innovative and more efficient, leveraging both my creativity and my expertise.

I started coding with Logo 28 years ago. I've been participating to Java projets since 1995, as Adesoft's R&D leader, as Java and Agile consultant at Valtech, CTO at Algodeal, a hedge fund and then developer at SonarSource, the company behind Sonar.

In 2010, I took the leadership on open-sourced Infinitest. An Eclipse and IntelliJ plugin that runs unit tests as soon as changes are made to the source code. Infinitest is the dream come true for TDD addicts because it dramatically reduces feedback loops.

You might be a reader of my blog JavaBien! since 2004.
